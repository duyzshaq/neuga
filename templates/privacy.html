Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Neuga AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website.

2. Data We Collect

We do not store your chat history permanently. Conversations are processed in real-time to provide responses and are discarded shortly after the session ends.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party APIs (DeepSeek and DuckDuckGo) to provide AI and search capabilities. Anonymized query data may be processed by these services to fulfill your requests.

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the functioning of the website (e.g., to maintain session state). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@neuga.ai.
